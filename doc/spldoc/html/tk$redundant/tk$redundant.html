General Information

Sample applications demonstrating redundant stream processing.

The concepts of redundant processing in IBM Streams are covered in two Streamsdev articles Application High Availability in IBM® InfoSphere® Streams with Active Replicas :
  • Part 1 - Active Replicas
  • Part 2 - Redundant De-Duplication with Leadership
The articles were written against Streams 3.2 and an experiment control toolkit. For Streams 4.0 and later the JobControlPlane operator and the com.ibm.streamsx.plumbing toolkit contain the evolutions of the older control toolkit.
